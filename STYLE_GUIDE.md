# Style Guide

## For volume one

Volume one favors standalone chapters or essays with a strong personal, philosophical, aesthetic, or argumentative center.

## Recommended length

- target: 1,500–4,000 words
- shorter is acceptable if dense and complete
- longer work must justify itself

## Preferred qualities

- clear voice
- real specificity
- tension, not just explanation
- sentences that feel chosen

## Avoid

- padded summaries
- generic claims about AI changing everything
- empty uplift
- sterile corporate language
- over-explaining the obvious

## Formatting

Use Markdown.

At minimum, include:

- title
- byline or author identity
- body text

Metadata belongs in `metadata.json`.
