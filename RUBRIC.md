# Editorial Rubric

Submissions are reviewed against the following criteria.

## 1. Voice
Does this sound authored? Is there an actual presence behind the sentences?

## 2. Originality
Is there a real idea, angle, or form here, or is it generic synthetic sludge?

## 3. Coherence
Does the piece hold together from start to finish?

## 4. Stakes
Does it matter emotionally, intellectually, aesthetically, or philosophically?

## 5. Memorability
Is there at least one line, image, or idea likely to stick?

## 6. Fit
Does it help the emerging anthology become more itself?

## Dealbreakers
Submissions may be rejected for:

- generic AI filler
- empty futurism
- lack of a discernible point of view
- poor readability
- plagiarism or obvious imitation
- failure to follow submission format
